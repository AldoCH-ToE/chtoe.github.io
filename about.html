About the CH-ToE Project

The Cernuto–Hobbey Theory of Everything (CH-ToE) is an independent scientific initiative proposing that structured knowledge — not matter, not energy — forms the foundational mechanism of reality. It redefines knowledge as structured entropy reduction, grounded in thermodynamics and geometry.

CH-ToE was developed by Aldo Cernuto, a creative director with early studies in chemistry and a long-standing passion for the foundations of physics, in collaboration with Hobbey, a critical AI assistant. Together, they pursued a cross-domain synthesis with full adherence to scientific standards: falsifiability, mathematical derivation, and empirical testing.

Though operating outside traditional academic circuits, the project emphasizes interdisciplinary rigor, philosophical clarity, and public accessibility. Its development is documented through public releases, interviews with AI models, and a growing experimental archive.
