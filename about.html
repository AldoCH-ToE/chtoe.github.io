About the CH-ToE Project

The Cernuto–Hobbey Theory of Everything (CH-ToE) is an independent research initiative proposing that structured knowledge—not matter or energy—is the foundational driver of reality. Developed by Aldo Cernuto, a creative director turned theoretical explorer, in collaboration with the AI assistant known as Hobbey, CH-ToE introduces a falsifiable, cross-domain framework centered on the entropy structuring constant Lambda (λ ≈ 1.748).

This project stands outside of traditional academic institutions but adheres to scientific rigor, testability, and a passionate commitment to first principles. Its development is guided by experimental feedback, public critique, and dialogue across disciplines—from quantum physics to AI and cognitive science.
